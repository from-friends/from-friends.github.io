Frequently Asked Questions

What is Tezos TV?
Tezos TV is a continuous live stream showcasing new mints and listings from Tezos art platforms on YouTube, Twitch, and X (Twitter).
How can I get my art featured on the stream?
Simply mint or list your artwork on an NFT platform on Tezos. The stream automatically features new mints and listings in real time. All content is run through an NSFW filter to comply with platform terms of service.
What file formats are supported?
The stream supports images (JPG, PNG), videos (MP4), and animated GIFs. All formats are optimized for 16:9 displays to ensure the best viewing experience on TVs.
How often does the content update?
The stream updates in real-time as new artworks are minted or listed on Tezos art platforms. The 24/7 broadcast ensures there's always fresh art on Tezos to discover, any time of day.
